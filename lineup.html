One artist, one dish, one unforgettable night — four days of pure magic.

The Groove Collective

Funk · Soul · Live Band

A seven-piece powerhouse bringing high-energy funk and deep soul to the main stage. Expect non-stop dancing, brass horns, and grooves you won't shake for days.

🎵 Music 🎤 Main Stage

Example Ceviche Tostada

Example description — fresh tiger prawn ceviche on a crispy corn tostada, with avocado, pickled red onion and a hint of lime. Bright, zesty, and impossible to put down.

👨‍🍳 by Chef Lucia Fernández
🌾 Gluten-Free

21:00 → 23:00

Elena Vasquez

Jazz · Latin · Bossa Nova

Elena's sultry saxophone weaves between jazz, bossa nova and Latin rhythms in a way that feels both intimate and cinematic. A performance not to be missed under the open sky.

🎵 Music 🎤 Main Stage
🍽️ Dish of the day

Example Birria Tacos

Example description — braised beef birria tacos with melted cheese, rich consommé dipping broth, diced onion and fresh cilantro. A festival favourite, served hot all evening long.

👨‍🍳 by Chef Carlos Mendez
🌶️ Spicy
🥁
21:00 → 23:00

Drumline Riot

Percussion · World · Afrobeat

Twenty drummers, one unstoppable energy. Drumline Riot fuses West African rhythms with contemporary percussion into a full-body sonic experience that shakes the ground beneath you.

🎵 Music 🎤 Main Stage
🍽️ Dish of the day · Closing Night

Example Midnight Chocolate Fondue

Example description — dark Belgian chocolate fondue served with fresh fruit, toasted marshmallows and salted caramel biscuits. The sweetest possible way to close four incredible days.

👨‍🍳 by Chef Sophie Laurent
🌿 Vegetarian
🎤
21:00 → 23:00

The Neon Wolves

Indie Rock · Alternative · Closing Set

Closing the festival with a bang, The Neon Wolves deliver an anthemic set of indie rock and electric alternative. Four days end here — and they'll make sure you never forget it.
